JAN DE COSTER

Jan De Coster is artist and robot designer renowned for crafting robots, interactive installations, and prototypes that bridge advertising, art, and scientific exploration. With over two decades of experience, he has served as an art director and producer for prestigious agencies like BBDO, TBWA, and Boondoggle.

"Unbreakable Houdini" by Will MacNeil

Digital Artist Will MacNeil from The Mill is on a quest to find new ways to tell stories visually. And Houdini is his loved and trusted horse. In this talk, Will looks at his latest projects which range from a basic system for creating rips in paper to a realtime system for visualising brainwaves from an EEG. He'll also talk about his determination to find meaningful and ethical uses for machine learning tools and demo a few of these. And of course Will will also drop in a few of the Mill's latest Houdini jobs. There will be something for here anyone interested in Houdini, generative design or AI.
